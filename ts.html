Long long back Gururaj had explained me tattvasankhyana. Let me copy paste it here ğŸ˜Š
The first level of classification is
  • Swatantra tattva or Independent principles..Lord Vishnu alone is Swatandra
  • Aswatandra tattva - Dependent principles - Everyone else is dependent on Vishnu
Rest all are copy pasted from Gururajaru's messages
Among the dependent principles, there are broadly two types
Bhava=that which exists
Abhava=That which doesn't exist
Bhava again is further classified sentient(chetana), insentient(achetana) and it is again classified
All that actually "exist" are bhaava....for example, jivas,space, time etc..
That is easier to understand
But abhava, is something which is not existent
It has three types
1)praag abhaava= say there is a pot...before the pot came into existence there was its *absence*...this is prAgabhAva
2)pradhvamsAbhava= after the pot is destroyed, that *absence* is given by this word
3)sad abhAva= That which *never* exists...for example the hare's horns or a flower in the sky
When v see a object, we by default perceive that object as *different* from all others....Thus *difference* is in the very essence of every object...this itself is called anyonya abhava
